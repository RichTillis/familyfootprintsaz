Frequently Asked Questions

How do I begin my search?

First, a (free) phone consultation is required. During the consultation, your questions are answered, including what your legal rights are and how to proceed with your search.

How long does the search process take?

Each post adoption search has its own unique set of circumstances and challenges, and can take anywhere from several weeks to many months to find the answers and parties being searched. I will maintain ongoing contact with you throughout your search to keep you abreast of the search progress.

What if the person being searched for is deceased or doesn’t want to be contacted?

If the person you are searching for is deceased, I will share this information with you and will attempt to make contact with any surviving relatives allowed under the Arizona statutes guidelines without additional costs to you.

If the person you are searching for is contacted and expresses that they do not wish to authorize the release of information or have further contact, then their wishes for anonymity must be honored and I am unable, by law, to make any further contact.
